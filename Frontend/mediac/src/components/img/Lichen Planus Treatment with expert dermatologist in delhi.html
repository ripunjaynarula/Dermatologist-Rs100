Lichen Planus

What is lichen planus?

Lichen planus is a skin condition which is non-infectious, itchy and can affect any part of the body eg. arms, legs, mouth.

It is associated with immune system.

What are the symptoms of lichen planus?

  • Purple, pink coloured flat to elevated lesion
  • Itching is severe
  • Lesions in the mouth cause burning sensation

What causes lichen planus?

  • It is an autoimmune disorder
  • It can be associated with medicine intake
  • Some dental products
  • Hepatitis-c

How to prevent this condition?

  • Avoid stress
  • Prevent injury
  • Avoid scratching
  • Apply soothing agents on the skin for preventing mouth Lichen planus
  • Avoid smoking, chewing tobacco & alcohol intake
  • Maintain oral hygiene
  • Rule out oral cancer
